Fubu x One Music Fest

Social media posts have different forms: text posts, polls, feed posts or stories, to name a few. I suggested that Fubu limit their promotional posts strictly to stories; some posts don’t need a long lifespan, especially when their soul duty is to deliver information once, which will not be relevant after a certain time. So, I created two animation posts to be posted as Instagram stories, 1080px by 1920px, one with animating typography and another with an edit of videos taken from a past year’s One Music Fest.
