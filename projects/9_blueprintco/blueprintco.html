Blueprint + Co

Part of my responsibilities at the Shark Group was constructing brand decks for clients. The depth would range depending on the purpose, a long and detailed brand deck would be designed for the client themselves, and sometimes a condensed version would also be created to be used as a reference for future clients the company would venture out to. These are branding visuals and mockups used in a brand deck I build for Blueprint + Co, a coworking space in midtown, Manhattan.
