Daymond John: Holiday Card

One of my final tasks as an intern for Daymond John was designing the 2019 holiday card. The designs in the past included figurines made of clay and illustrations of Daymond and his fellow sharks – this year we created sock puppets of the Shark Tanks panel, using Bombas Socks, a client of Daymond’s. With that said, it was an obvious call to push the DIY aesthetic all the way through, which included: a cardboard cut-out tree and Stair of David, red and green Christmas ornaments printed on stock, miniature gifts with shark wrapping paper and red tags, mini pinecones, artificial snow and, of course, Christmas lights.
I also suggest we create a digital card. While at the Shark Group I was able to work more on animations and motion grafts and thought those learns could be implemented on the project
