Mother Courage and Her Children

Tagline: Any means necessary. During my third year at Parsons, my collegue, Vincent Garcia, and I planned and designed a print campaign for a hypothetical rendition on Bertolt Brecht's Mother Courage and Her Children. Grungy, tattered and kinetic, were the adjectives we wanted to illustrate our contemporary spin on a german classic.
