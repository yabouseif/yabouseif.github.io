Hatch: Your Social Life

“The easiest way to make your new favorite memories. Experience the things you love with the people around you.” Hatch is a social networking app that allows you to create and join plans seamlessly, by curating your social circle and blasting events to a select number of friends. Plans could be as vague as a “move night this weekend”, or detailed to the time, place and capacity. Visit heyhatch.com, or find us on the App Store, and let “sometime” be this time.
