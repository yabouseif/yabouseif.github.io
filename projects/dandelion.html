The Dandelion Brush

"Go to CVS and pick out a product under $5." Cotton Swabs. "Pick a colour." Red. "Give it a new name. Give it a new function. Give it a brand." Hense, The Dandelion Brush. From the Latin dens leonis, translating to "lion’s tooth": a tribute to Leo Gerstenzang, the maker of the cotton swab. I invisioned the rebrand to incorporate a sence of delicacy, transparency as well as extensiveness. I then designed a social media campaign, consisting of nine posts: three showing the product only, three text only, and three product and text. Each of those three categories includes an animation while integrating the colour red. Download all nine deliverables here.
